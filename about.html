What's a Pug?

A Pug is a very distinctive breed of dog. With its classic squished face and wrinkled breadloaf body, it has become a favorite of many across the world. The breed originally hails from China, and was considered the main companion of ancient Chinese Emporers, they eventually made their way over to Holland serving as the mascot for their royal House of Orange. Today, they are a breed that we see walking down the street all the time.
