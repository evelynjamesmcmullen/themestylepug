Adoptable Pugs

Petfinder

Websites like petfinder are great resources for finding rescue pugs for adoption.

American Kennel Club

The American Kennel Club offers a search engine that is helpful for finding reputable breeders in your area. if you do choose to buy instead of adopt, at least make sure that you are buying from an ethical breeder.

Your Local Shelter

So many pugs, among other breeds, are dropped off at shelters every day. Make sure that you use your local shelter as an option while looking for pugs. Rescue dogs are often already housetrained and make an excellent addition to any family.
